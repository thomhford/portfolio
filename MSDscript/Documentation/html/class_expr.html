◆ pretty_print()

void Expr::pretty_print ( std::ostream &  ot)

Like print, pretty_print takes a std::ostream& argument and prints there. Unlike print, pretty_print includes a space around + or *, and it avoids unnecessary parentheses by relying the usual precedence rules for multiplication and addition. It can also avoid parentheses by assuming that operators associate to the right. -Calls pretty_print_at() where specific printing for methods takes place letParenth determines whether let expressions will contain parenthesis.
